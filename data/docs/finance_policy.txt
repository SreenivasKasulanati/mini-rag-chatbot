Title: Finance Policy (Demo)

1. Purpose
This document describes demo finance policies for a hypothetical organization.

2. Reimbursements
Employees must submit receipts within 30 days of purchase. Approval is required for expenses over $500.

3. Travel
- Domestic flights should be booked at least 14 days in advance.
- Hotel costs should be mid-tier business class.
- Daily meal per diem is $60.

4. Procurement
Purchases above $1,000 require two vendor quotes. The finance team reviews contracts.

5. Reporting
Monthly summaries are due by the 5th of the following month.

6. Contacts
finance@example.org for questions.

7. Leaves
One can take at least 12 days leave in a year.
